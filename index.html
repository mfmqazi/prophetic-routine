About This Project

This website is dedicated to reviving the Sunnah by presenting the daily life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in an accessible and beautiful format.

All information is derived from authentic Sunni sources, primarily Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, and Al-Shamail Al-Muhammadiyyah by Imam Tirmidhi.

Our goal is to help Muslims connect with their Prophet (PBUH) by knowing his schedule, his habits, and his noble character.

Created with ❤️ to honor the Sunnah.
